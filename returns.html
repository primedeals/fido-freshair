Return Policy

90-Day Money-Back Guarantee

At Crayford Digital LLC, we stand behind our products with a 90-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we'll gladly refund your money.

Return Process

  • Contact us within 90 days of receiving your product
  • Email contact@crayforddigital.com to initiate your return
  • Include your order number and reason for return
  • We'll provide return shipping instructions

Refund Information

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Return Conditions

  • Products must be in their original packaging
  • Digital products and downloadable items are non-refundable
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the product was defective

For specific questions about returns, please contact our customer service team at contact@crayforddigital.com
